## Overview

Core is Libraries for Xeus, Lxna and others.

## Goals

- Xeus, Lxnaでの共通処理を分離し、再利用可能にすること。

## Features

- Serialize and Deserialize (Format is `RocketPack`)
- RPC (`RocketPack` base)
